#### **:blue[User Checklist]** 

**All users, please make sure your directory information is accurate. Use the checklist below:**

- [ ] My profile is in the directory (check the *Staff Directory* under *Directories*) 
- [ ] My profile contains a headshot photo (check *FAQ* under *Resources* on how to update your headshot)  

**My profile information is up-to-date and accurate:**
- [ ] Name 
- [ ] Job Title
- [ ] :green-badge[Position] / :blue-badge[Assigned Unit] / :orange-badge[Office Location] / :violet-badge[Birthday] 
- [ ] Work Phone # 
- [ ] Work Email Address (@jacksongov.org)
- [ ] Personal Phone #

If you do not see your profile in the directory, that means you have not yet been entered into our directory! Please reach out to both [Candace Mims](mailto:candace.mims@jacksongov.org) and [Joseph Cho](mailto:ujcho@jacksongov.org) to complete the personnal intake survey.
