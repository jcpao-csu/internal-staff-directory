To add your office headshot photo to your Microsoft org account:  

1. Access your Outlook [**online**](https://outlook.office.com/mail/)  
2. Click your account (if no photo profile uploaded, should looks like a circle icon with your initials), located on the very top right-hand corner  
3. Click "My Microsoft 365 profile"  
4. "Update your profile"  
5. Edit "profile picture"   
6. Upload photo and save  

Alternatively, via Microsoft Teams:  

1. Click your account (if no photo profile uploaded, should looks like a circle icon with your initials), located on the very top right-hand corner  
2. Click "View Account"  
3. In org user account profile, click profile picture and add photo   
4. Upload photo and save   

Or, change your profile photo [**HERE**](https://myaccount.microsoft.com/?ref=MeControl)   

View this [**link**](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/add-your-profile-photo-to-microsoft-365-2eaf93fd-b3f1-43b9-9cdc-bdcd548435b7) for further questions
